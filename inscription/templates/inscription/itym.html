    International Tournament of Young Mathematicians

    Le tournoi international des jeunes mathématiciens est l'équivalent international du TFJM.

    The ITYM is a team competition for high school students from all over the world. Its purpose is to stimulate interest in mathematics and its applications, to develop scientific thinking, communication skills and teamwork. The ITYM is a mathematics competition, but it is very different from most other contests and olympiads. Its mathematics, and the way it is organized, tries to convey a spirit of research mathematics as much as possible, in many ways: participation is in teams (at most six students), there is ample time to work on the problems (they are published several months in advance), the problems contain parts without known solution, and students present the solutions to each other (in the spirit of “peer-reviewing” ‑ this is a sort of research debate involving Reporter, Opponent, Reviewer and Observer).

    From ITYM's website

    En 2014, il s'est déroulé à Brême en Allemagne où les deux meilleures équipes du TFJM y ont participé. Les vainqueurs de l'ITYM 2014 sont l'équipe qui a obtenu le 2ème prix au TFJM. En 2015 l'ITYM aura lieu à Sofia, en Bulgarie là où les meilleures équipes françaises participeront à la compétition. Les problèmes de l'ITYM 2015 sont déjà en ligne !
